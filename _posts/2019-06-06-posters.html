Posters are fun. There’s a little more freedom with the imagery when you’re not selling a product or establishing a brand. It’s temporary and intended to delight. You have to catch the eye and communicate the info quickly. People are going to glance at this rather than spending time reading it. I make use of text hierarchy to make a poster read well and communicate efficiently.
