The Prompt:

Sexy Pizza is turning 10 years old! They needed a t-shirt design for their employees and an ad to match.

My Solution:

I pitched a few ideas for the subject of this t-shirt. "Lady passionately kissing pizza" won. I adopted the Sexy Pizza logo style and drew inspiration from 50's comic illustrations. I found pics of passionate kissing to model from. I created this illustration in vector format so that it can be scaled and edited easily.
