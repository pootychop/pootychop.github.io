The Whittier Bar themed themselves as a classy dive. In the Whittier district of Tulsa, OK, this bar represents a nod to its past, a bar for the people. They’ve upgraded ever so slightly to appease the public’s rising appreciation for the culinary arts. This bar serves high-class drinks. Red leather upholstered paneling decorates the bar. This padded diamond pattern really echoes the essence of the bar, so I used it in the design.
